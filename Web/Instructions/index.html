SPEED
Instructions:
Speed is a 2 Player game, where each Player competes to empty their decks, each consisting of 26 cards.

At the start of the Game, 4 Cards are taken from the Players' decks, and displayed to both Players. Additionally, at the start of each New Round, a card is taken from both players and placed in the centre as 2 different piles.

To place a card, you need to select a card among the 4 they have (at the bottom of the screen), and then clicking one of the cards in the centre; the card can only be placed if the centre card chosen is consecutive in number (1 more or 1 less); in this game, Ace and King are considered consecutive.

The aim is to be as quick to play, as your opponent is also given the chance to place cards in the time you waste. Once a card is placed, you may click the Deck on the right of your cards, to refill the empty slots.

Once there are no moves you are able to play you may click on the button labelled 'End Round', and wait till your opponent does the same; a New Round will then start, beginning with the setting out of a card from each Player's deck. If your deck has been emptied, you would need to choose a card before clicking the button.
